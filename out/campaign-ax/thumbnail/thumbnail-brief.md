# YouTube Thumbnail Brief — "Your Horoscope App Is Guessing"

## Concept
Split-screen confrontation: left = generic Western horoscope app UI (washed out, grey, "Gemini ♊ Today"), right = SajuMuse dark mystic reading with four glowing pillars 年月日時. Between them, a vertical gold seam of light.

## Composition (left→right)
- **Left 45%**: desaturated grey phone mockup, fake Co-Star-style one-liner, small red "GUESSING" stamp rotated -8°
- **Center seam**: thin gold vertical lightbeam, small crescent moon midpoint
- **Right 55%**: deep indigo/black field, four vertical gold hanja columns 年 月 日 時 glowing, subtle star particles

## Text overlay

  > **YOUR HOROSCOPE**
  > **APP IS GUESSING.**
- Bottom-right, small caps gold `#F59E0B`:
  > WE READ YOUR FOUR PILLARS →

## Face / talent
- Optional: creator headshot bottom-left, expression = skeptical raised eyebrow (classic YT CTR driver). Otherwise omit — text-and-pillars composition stands alone.

## Technical
- Safe zone: keep text 80px from edges (YT timestamp overlay bottom-right)
- Contrast check: headline must read at 120px thumbnail size
- Palette: `#0A0A0F` · `#7C3AED` · `#F59E0B` · `#F5F1E8` · accent red `#DC2626` for stamp
