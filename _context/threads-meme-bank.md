# Threads Meme Bank — Ten Gods × Situations

> Recurring supply for Noon Meme slot. Each cell is a mineable template. threads-producer picks one cell per day, rewrites verbatim or in variation, rotates so no template appears twice within 30 days.

Terminology locked to `saju-engine-bridge.md`:
- Peer (비견) · Rival (겁재) · Output (식신) · Expression (상관) · Wealth–Windfall (편재) · Wealth–Steady (정재) · Power–Pressure (편관) · Power–Authority (정관) · Resource–Unconventional (편인) · Resource–Nurturing (정인)

## Matrix (Ten Gods × 7 situations = 70 seed templates)

Columns: **Work / Friendship / Romance / Family / Money / Health / Self-talk**

### Peer (비견)
- Work: *teams up fast, runs into identity clash when coworker is also Peer-heavy*
- Friendship: *"i found my twin" energy, sometimes too much*
- Romance: *picks someone who mirrors them; forgets to import new information*
- Family: *quietly competes with sibling of same element*
- Money: *spends like their friend group*
- Health: *overtrains in group settings, undertrains alone*
- Self-talk: *"i know who i am" — usually true*

### Rival (겁재)
- Work: *best in competitive environments, chaotic in cooperative ones*
- Friendship: *loyal to 3 people, skeptical of everyone else*
- Romance: *attracts intensity, survives it*
- Family: *the sibling your parents quietly worry about — unfairly*
- Money: *spends faster than they earn, earns faster than they plan*
- Health: *ignores tired until it's a diagnosis*
- Self-talk: *"i'm fine" — louder than necessary*

### Output (식신)
- Work: *makes things. a lot of things. shows up at deadline with a notebook full*
- Friendship: *the one who sends voice notes*
- Romance: *courts with creation — playlists, meals, handmade anything*
- Family: *the chosen one for family group chat memes*
- Money: *spends on supplies, forgets to invoice*
- Health: *needs to move — mental health tied to body movement*
- Self-talk: *"what if i just made the thing" — often the right answer*

### Expression (상관)
- Work: *tells the truth at meetings. survives it. barely.*
- Friendship: *the one you call when you need the unvarnished version*
- Romance: *too honest on dates 1–3; exactly right by date 5*
- Family: *the family's unofficial therapist, without permission*
- Money: *negotiates aggressively; occasionally wins*
- Health: *talks their way through pain, actually helpful*
- Self-talk: *"i'm not mad i just —" (they're a little mad)*

### Wealth–Windfall (편재)
- Work: *opportunity-spotter, hates routine, leaves good jobs too early*
- Friendship: *knows everyone. actually knows 3*
- Romance: *falls in love fast, usually with potential*
- Family: *the favorite uncle/aunt energy, even at 22*
- Money: *money arrives, money leaves, money comes back*
- Health: *feast or fast, never between*
- Self-talk: *"something's coming" — often correct, rarely on schedule*

### Wealth–Steady (정재)
- Work: *builds slowly, outlasts everyone*
- Friendship: *10-year friendships, low drama, high trust*
- Romance: *long-term from the start, even in their 20s*
- Family: *the responsible one, sometimes resents it*
- Money: *spreadsheet user*
- Health: *has a routine and sticks to it*
- Self-talk: *"this is fine" — usually actually is*

### Power–Pressure (편관)
- Work: *performs under deadline like oxygen depends on it*
- Friendship: *the one who holds everyone's crisis*
- Romance: *magnet for intensity, exhausted by it*
- Family: *the oldest daughter archetype regardless of birth order*
- Money: *earns under pressure, can't save in calm*
- Health: *chronic tension, wakes tight-shouldered*
- Self-talk: *"i can handle it" — true, unsustainable*

### Power–Authority (정관)
- Work: *wants clear structure, good manager energy*
- Friendship: *the planner*
- Romance: *traditional arc, not traditional person*
- Family: *keeps the family calendar in their head*
- Money: *budgets, invests, compounds*
- Health: *annual physicals, knows their vitamin D level*
- Self-talk: *"what's the right way" — good question, sometimes a trap*

### Resource–Unconventional (편인)
- Work: *niche expertise, weird career path that makes sense at the end*
- Friendship: *one deep friendship beats ten shallow*
- Romance: *attracted to strange, misread as picky*
- Family: *the different child*
- Money: *random windfalls from unlikely sources*
- Health: *eastern + western medicine, combines both*
- Self-talk: *"i see it differently" — you actually do*

### Resource–Nurturing (정인)
- Work: *long learners, good teachers, slow to switch fields*
- Friendship: *the mom of the friend group, chosen*
- Romance: *safe love, earned over time*
- Family: *parental energy since childhood*
- Money: *inheritance-mindset even without inheritance*
- Health: *sleeps well, eats well, still worries*
- Self-talk: *"am i doing enough" — often yes, rarely felt*

## Micro-variations (format patterns)

When rewriting a cell for a post, pick one lens:

- **POV**: "Power-Pressure types when [scenario]:"
- **Contrast**: "Western astrology: [X] / Four Pillars: [Y]"
- **Self-recognition**: "weak Day Master reading their chart: [reaction]"
- **Relational**: "[Ten God] × [Ten God] friendship: [pattern]"
- **Timing**: "[month pillar] + [Day Master] = [observation]"

---

## Rotation rules (for threads-producer)
- No single cell reused within **30 days**.
- No more than **2 posts on the same Ten God** in one week.
- Mix Ten Gods proportional to actual audience: 40% Power (Pressure+Authority), 25% Output/Expression, 20% Wealth, 15% others. (Power-types are disproportionately over-represented among GenZ seekers.)
- Never use a meme in a week when an engine-backed chart read of the same Ten God is scheduled — conflict of framing.
